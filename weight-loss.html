Hatha Yoga

Basic and most popular type of yoga, focusing on yoga postures (asanas) and breathing exercises (pranayama). Suitable for everyone at all levels, from beginners to experienced practitioners.

Hatha Yoga

Basic and most popular type of yoga, focusing on yoga postures (asanas) and breathing exercises (pranayama). Suitable for everyone at all levels, from beginners to experienced practitioners.

Ashtanga Yoga

Has a fixed sequence of yoga postures that are performed in a specific order. Requires high levels of discipline and focus, but also offers many health benefits, including increased strength, flexibility, and endurance.

Ashtanga Yoga

Has a fixed sequence of yoga postures that are performed in a specific order. Requires high levels of discipline and focus, but also offers many health benefits, including increased strength, flexibility, and endurance.

Bikram Yoga

Bikram Yoga, also known as "Hot Yoga," is a sequence of 26 postures and 2 breathing exercises performed in a high-temperature room (around 40°C). It helps the body detoxify and enhances flexibility.

Bikram Yoga

Bikram Yoga, also known as "Hot Yoga," is a sequence of 26 postures and 2 breathing exercises performed in a high-temperature room (around 40°C). It helps the body detoxify and enhances flexibility.

Vinyasa Yoga

Links yoga postures together in a flowing sequence with the breath, creating a continuous flow of movement. Requires more strength and stamina than Hatha yoga, but also offers many health benefits, including increased strength, flexibility, and endurance.

Vinyasa Yoga

Links yoga postures together in a flowing sequence with the breath, creating a continuous flow of movement. Requires more strength and stamina than Hatha yoga, but also offers many health benefits, including increased strength, flexibility, and endurance.

Yin Yoga

Focuses on passively stretching the muscles and connective tissues for long periods of time. Helps to relax the muscles, improve flexibility, and reduce stress.

Prenatal Yoga

Designed specifically for pregnant women. Helps to ease pregnancy symptoms, improve posture, and prepare for childbirth.

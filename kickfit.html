Diverse exercises for students

We offer a variety of kickfit exercises for students such as: Shadow Boxing, Pad Work, Bag Work, Technique Drills, Sparring, Self-Defense Techniques.

Shadow Boxing

In Kickfit, shadow boxing trains punches, kicks, and dodges solo, refining technique and movement. It improves flexibility, reflexes, and stamina.

Bag Work

Kickfit uses bag work (hitting a heavy bag) to train punches and kicks. This improves strength, technique, and stamina for kickboxing. It's essential for building power and endurance.

Sparring

Kickfit sparring puts your honed techniques to the test in a controlled fight simulation, refining your reflexes, timing, and fighting strategy.

Pad Work

In kickfit, pad work involves punches and kicks on pads held by a partner to improve accuracy, power, speed, and reflexes.

Technique Drills

Kickfit technique drills: Master punches, kicks, defense, and footwork. Become faster, more precise, and agile under expert guidance. Improve your fighting ability and self-defense skills.

Self-Defense Techniques

Kickfit incorporates self-defense techniques through drills that refine punches, kicks, and footwork, enhancing your ability to react and defend yourself.
